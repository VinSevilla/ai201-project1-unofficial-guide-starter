The Importance of Leetcode
The Importance of Leetcode
If you’re a developer or looking to be one, you’ve most likely heard the name “LeetCode” being thrown around more times than one can count.

That website everyone talks about, loved and cursed by many. But here is the real deal: why would you want to do LeetCode every single day?

I’m about to break down why just investing a small chunk of your time each day into LeetCode will make your coding game so much stronger in many ways.

If you are not a member, you can access to full text here.

1. Consistency Breeds Mastery
You’ve heard the saying, “Practice makes perfect”?

It really is true in the world of programming. You use it or you lose it. Doing problems on Leetcode daily helps to solidify problem-solving skills and you’ll be able to build a repository in your head.

Here’s a good starting point: this LeetCode Guide.

That way, you will be prepared for every coding challenge out there. Once you work on coding puzzles on a regular basis, patterns start to emerge.

You will begin to see patterns in both algorithms and data structures, and interviews or real-world problem-solving start to feel a lot less like scaling Everest and a lot more like a brisk walk.

2. It Sharpens Your Analytical Thinking
Perhaps more valuable than knowing the syntax of any one language is knowing how to actually think like a programmer.

The bottom line is that through regularly solving LeetCode problems, you are training your brain to think about any issue with an analytical mindset. You learn how to break daunting problems into smaller pieces and apply step-by-step logical reasoning.

And let me tell you, this mental workout will pay off far beyond coding interviews. You’ll find that your debugging, code optimization, and critical thinking skills have greatly improved in your day-to-day work.

3. The Key to Acing Interviews
We’re all aware that most tech interviews out there include solving algorithmic challenges.

These problems are there to test your problem-solving skills along with your grasp of basic computer science concepts. If you want to go deeper, this could be a pretty useful comprehensive guide to interview algorithms. And the advantage of incorporating Leetcode into your daily routine is that you’re essentially studying well in advance for interviews before ever having to.

But when that big interview day comes around, you will be confident to take it head-on, for the solution of Leetcode problems has become a piece of cake.

4. Leetcode Teaches You the Art of Optimization
Honestly speaking, in many situations where the dataset is quite large or the system should work in real time, writing code which “works” isn’t enough.

Then your code should be efficient. LeetCode not only teaches you problem-solving but also finding the most optimal one. You will learn to appreciate the distinctions like O(n) vs O(n²) and develop the instinct of finding out where the code is inefficient.

For example, a search for an element in an unsorted array may involve O(n) time while some nested operations-like paired sum in an array-find pairs whose sum comes up to a target-may be O(n²).

More on that, see this nice comparison of algorithmic complexities. It’s one of those skills that sets you apart from others.

5. Daily Practice Builds Confidence
Ever get that sinking feeling when you’re faced with a problem and you have absolutely no idea where to start?

LeetCode helps eradicate that fear by exposing you over time to a wide variety of problems. And the more you practice each day, the more confident you become to handle the unknown.

The confidence attained does not just apply to interviews, it carries on into your projects, your team discussions, even to your personal coding ventures.

Conclusion
To that end, the question of whether daily LeetCode practice is worth it sees a yes for an answer.

Its benefits go far beyond merely passing the coding interviews to making you a more proficient, analytical developer, full of confidence. Go ahead and pick up a problem today-your future self will be grateful!

Have a wonderful day!
